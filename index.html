Our Heritage

Three generations of
maple craft.

Founded in 1978 by Wayne Lytton, Maple Terroir is now led by his son Shawn as President. Our family's connection to the land runs deep — we source exclusively from a fourth-generation maple farm nestled in the Appalachian Mountains of Quebec.

The Lytton Family

From the forests of Quebec
to tables around the world.

What began as a single family's passion for pure maple has grown into a brand trusted across Canada, Japan, Korea, and China. We've never wavered from our founding principle: quality over quantity, always.

An Artistic Lineage

Founder Wayne Lytton's grandmother, Georgina Abrams Lytton, was a celebrated artist whose work resides in the National Gallery of Canada. That same eye for craft and beauty runs through everything we create.

Proudly Canadian

Based in Vancouver, British Columbia. Made in Canada using mainly Canadian ingredients. Serving families and retailers across Canada and Asia since 1978.

Single-Origin Terroir

The Appalachian
Mountains of Quebec.

Terroir isn't just for wine. The character of our maple syrup is shaped by the unique microclimate of Quebec's Appalachian range — cold winters that concentrate the sap, mineral-rich soils that deepen the flavour, and old-growth forests where the sugar maples have stood for generations.

We harvest late in the season, when the sap runs deepest and richest. No blending, no shortcuts. Every bottle traces back to one farm, one family, one forest.
