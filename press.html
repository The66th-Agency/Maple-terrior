Press & Media

A Canadian maple family,
ready to talk terroir.

Maple Terroir is a third-generation family business sourcing single-origin, certified organic maple syrup from the Appalachian Mountains of Quebec. Founded in 1978 by Wayne Lytton and now led by his son Shawn, we welcome interview requests, product features, and collaborations.

Fact Sheet

Quick facts
for your story.

Founded
1978 by Wayne Lytton. Now led by his son Shawn as President. Three generations of the Lytton family running the business.
Headquarters
9291 Shaughnessy Street, Vancouver, BC V6P 6P4 (Canadian distribution and tasting room).
Origin
Single-origin syrup sourced from one farm in the Appalachian Mountains of Quebec.
Certifications
Ecocert, Canada Organic, and USDA Organic (certifications apply to the maple syrup).
Signature Product
Maple-leaf-shaped 100ml glass bottles. Also stroopwafels, chocolates, cookies, tea and coffee, and gift sets made with our maple syrup.
Harvest Window
Late-harvest tap, yielding a darker, fuller-bodied syrup with pronounced caramel and mineral notes.
Distribution
Direct-to-consumer via mapleterroir.com and select wholesale partners across several international markets.
