Verified Quality

The Certifications Behind Every Box

Maple Terroir maple syrup carries three independent organic certifications, each verified annually by a separate accrediting body.

Ecocert

European organic certification, recognised across global premium retail and food service channels.

Canada Organic

The Canadian Food Inspection Agency's standard. Mandatory for organic claims on Canadian retail shelves.

USDA Organic

The US Department of Agriculture standard. Required for American retail and relevant to recipients throughout North America.

Organic certification applies to Maple Terroir maple syrup. Other products in the line, including stroopwafels, cookies, and confections, are made with certified organic maple syrup as an ingredient but are not certified organic as finished products.
