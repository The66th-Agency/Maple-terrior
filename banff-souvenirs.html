Why Maple

A Rockies Trip, A Canadian Gift That Lasts.

Banff sits in Alberta, far from the sugar maples of the east, but maple is still the Canadian souvenir with the most meaning. Canada produces most of the world supply, Quebec leads it, and maple is the flavour the whole country is known for, so it carries the trip home better than a generic gift-shop item.

Maple Terroir is the single-origin choice: one farm, one forest, a documented harvest, rather than a blend. That is what makes it a real gift rather than a grocery bottle.

The range works for any traveller: maple syrup, maple stroopwafels, maple candy and sugar, maple cream, and gift sets. Everything keeps for a long time and ships well, so you can send it home from Banff instead of packing it.
