Frequently Asked Questions

Once opened, maple products should be refrigerated and consumed within 6 months. Unopened products can be stored at room temperature in a cool, dry place. Maple syrup can also be frozen for long-term storage without affecting quality.
Our maple syrup products are certified organic by Ecocert Canada, USDA Organic, Canada Organic, and JAS (Japan Agricultural Standards). Our confectionery products are made with certified organic maple syrup and non-GMO ingredients.
Yes! We ship to Canada, the United States, Japan, and South Korea. Free shipping is available on Canadian orders over $99 CAD. International shipping rates are calculated at checkout.
If you receive a damaged or defective product, contact us within 14 days of delivery for a full replacement or refund. Due to the nature of food products, we cannot accept returns on opened items.
Our pure maple syrup, maple sugar, and maple cream butter are naturally free from gluten, dairy, nuts, and soy. Our confectionery products (stroopwafels, cookies, chocolates) may contain wheat, dairy, eggs, soy, and nuts. Please check individual product labels for specific allergen information.
Canada Grade A Golden has a delicate, light flavor — perfect for drizzling and desserts. Amber has a rich, full-bodied taste ideal for everyday use. Dark is robust with intense maple character, great for cooking and baking. Very Dark has the strongest flavor and is best for recipes where you want maple to shine through other ingredients.
